Criminal Record Check

After clicking the button below, you will be directed to a new website to immediately start your criminal background check. Please follow the instructions on that site. A few things to remember before starting:

  1. Don't leave any gaps in your living history,
  2. The "current address" requested is for your physical address,
  3. List all criminal offences you were convicted of (if any) with exact wording,
  4. Complete the Electronic ID verification, as this is how the system separates you from others (only you will see this info),
  5. Once you click below to start the criminal record check you will have to finish it in a single web session.
